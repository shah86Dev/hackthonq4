# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive university textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook is designed to provide a thorough understanding of the intersection between artificial intelligence and physical systems, with a particular focus on humanoid robotics.

## About This Textbook

This textbook covers four major modules:

1. **ROS2**: Robot Operating System fundamentals and advanced concepts
2. **Gazebo & Unity**: Simulation environments for robotics development
3. **NVIDIA Isaac**: GPU-accelerated robotics development platform
4. **VLA Models**: Vision-Language-Action models for embodied AI

Each module contains multiple chapters that build upon each other, providing both theoretical knowledge and practical applications.

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Undergraduate and graduate students in robotics and AI
- Researchers working in embodied AI
- Engineers developing robotic systems
- Anyone interested in the intersection of AI and physical systems

## How to Use This Textbook

- Start with Chapter 1 if you're new to robotics
- Each chapter includes theory, practical labs, and quizzes
- Complete the labs in simulation before attempting real hardware
- Use the RAG chatbot for additional explanations and clarifications

## Prerequisites

- Basic programming knowledge (Python recommended)
- Understanding of linear algebra and calculus
- Familiarity with Linux command line (helpful but not required)
