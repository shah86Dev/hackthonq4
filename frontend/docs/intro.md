## Course Structure

This textbook is organized into 4 modules spanning 16 weeks:

### Module 1: Foundations of Physical AI (4 weeks)

- [Introduction to Physical AI](/docs/module1/1-introduction-to-physical-ai)
- [Sensorimotor Coordination](/docs/module1/1-sensorimotor-coordination)
- [Embodied Cognition](/docs/module1/1-embodied-cognition)
- [Learning in Physical Systems](/docs/module1/1-learning-in-physical-systems)

### Module 2: Humanoid Robotics Fundamentals (4 weeks)

- [Humanoid Robot Platforms](/docs/module2/2-humanoid-robot-platforms)
- [Locomotion and Gait Control](/docs/module2/2-locomotion-and-gait-control)
- [Manipulation and Grasping](/docs/module2/2-manipulation-and-grasping)
- [Human-Robot Interaction](/docs/module2/2-human-robot-interaction)

- [Multi-Robot Physical AI](/docs/module3/3-multi-robot-physical-ai)

- [Industrial Physical AI](/docs/module4/4-industrial-physical-ai)
- [Service Robotics](/docs/module4/4-service-robotics)
